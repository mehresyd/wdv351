Events

We would love for you to come and join us at our events we hold every week! They are a great way to get to know lots of peers from MSU's collage as well as some locals living on campus town. We love to book musicians and artists locally as we love to support the community here on MSU campus. While studying and relaxing may be the atmosphere at our coffee shop during the day the evenings we like to kick back and have some fun with beer, wine, good food and some amazing entertainment!

Interested at performing at our coffee shop? We would love to hear from you as we are always searching for new fun talent! All you have to do to book or request a function is contact us. We have alot of booking spots that fill up fast so we recommend to schedule or request your booking a month ahead of when you would like to perform.
