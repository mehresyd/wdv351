Menu

At our coffee house you'll find that it is not just coffee we prepare. We have a wide array of cafe items to suit your needs at any time of day! We believe in both quality and quanity. We roast our own coffee in house with the best coffee beans around the world! Not a coffee lover? Thats okay, we have a variety of food and drink items to choose from that won't leave you feeling left out. Our menu has many soups, sandwhiches, salads as well as our bakery case that is full of fresh pastries and goodies made around the clock. The best teas, wine, beers and more are also avaliable morning and night!
