About Us

We welcome you to Savanna's Coffee House.

Savanna's Coffee House was founded in 2006 by MSU graduate students Michel Bohmer and Sandy Thomas. Located in the heart of MSU's campus town, we strived to make Savanna's Coffee House a fun, funky, relaxing yet upbeat destination for all students and locals to enjoy! We serve only the best premium products from our fresh roasted coffee and espresso drinks, hot and cold brewed iced teas, grilled and cold served sandwiches, and homemade soups and bakery items made fresh everyday. We made it so there was a little something for everyone to enjoy.

We not only serve during the day but we also love to host many fun night events and perfomances. Stop by in the evening and enjoy some wine or beer and listen to some live entertainment in our theatre space with performances by many local artist and musicians. Have talent you want to show off? Contact us to book a function at our cafe. Both Sandy and Michel have always been passionate about coffee since their collage days and wanted to bring the same love of coffee to their coffee shop for other locals and college students to enjoy like Michel and Sandy do!

Since Michel and Sandy have always only wanted the best quality for their coffee, they soucre their own ethical coffee beans from around the world and roast them on site right to your coffee cup. Our finest coffee beans come from places as far as Colombia, Guatemala, Costa Rica, and Jamaica. These countries are known to have grown and source the best coffee beans around. Click here for more information on our ethically sourced coffee beans.


We hope you enjoy your times spent here at Savanna's Coffee House. Everyday we strive to improve to be the best coffee shop around for all of our beloved MSU students and locals. It is an honour to be able to be apart of the same campus where Sandy and Michel found friendship and a love for MSU and coffee. We have built a cafe we are proud of and are happy to serve all in our laid back beautiful coffee house. Coffee is our life and we hope you feel the same at Savanna's.


Cordially,

Savanna's owners and staff
